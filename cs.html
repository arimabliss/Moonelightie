Happy Birthday, Archita ❤️
Dear Archita,

Happy Birthday, meri jaan. ❤️

Pata nahi yeh letter kahan se shuru karun, kyunki jitna main feel karta hoon, utna shayad words kabhi express hi nahi kar paayenge.

Sabse pehle, thank you. Thank you meri life mein aane ke liye. Aapse milna meri life ke un moments mein se hai jise main kabhi replace nahi karna chahta. Aap sirf meri girlfriend nahi hain, aap meri peace hain, meri smile ka reason hain, aur woh insaan hain jinke saath har chhoti si baat bhi special lagti hai.

Aaj aapka birthday hai, aur meri bas ek hi dua hai ki aapki life hamesha khushiyon se bhari rahe. Aapki har wish poori ho, aapke har sapne ko pankh mile, aur jab bhi aap kabhi thak jaayein ya udaas hon, aapko hamesha yaad rahe ki ek banda hai jo hamesha aapke saath khada rahega.

Mujhe aapki smile bahut pasand hai. Sach bolun toh jab aap hansti hain na, toh lagta hai jaise duniya thodi aur acchi ho gayi. Aur jab aap dukhi hoti hain, toh dil karta hai kaash aapka saara dard main le sakun.

Main perfect nahi hoon. Shayad kabhi galtiyan karun, kabhi aapko hurt bhi kar doon bina chahe. Lekin ek baat ka promise karta hoon—main har din better banne ki koshish karunga. Aapke liye. Humare liye.

Mujhe nahi pata future mein life humein kahan le jaayegi, lekin itna zaroor pata hai ki jitni der tak meri saanse chalengi, main aapke liye dua karta rahunga. Bas itna chahta hoon ki aapki zindagi mein kabhi pyaar ki kami na ho, respect ki kami na ho, aur aap hamesha khud par proud feel karein.

Aaj ka din sirf aapka hai. Toh aaj tension nahi, overthinking nahi. Sirf smile kijiye, cake khaiye, photos lijiye, aur apna birthday poori tarah enjoy kijiye. Aap is duniya ki saari khushiyon ki haqdar hain.

Aur haan...

Agar kabhi kisi din aapko lage ki sab kuch against ja raha hai, toh bas itna yaad rakhiyega—main hamesha aapki side hoon.

Happy Birthday once again, meri Archita. ❤️

May this year bring endless happiness, success, peace, and countless beautiful memories. Stay the amazing, kind, caring, and beautiful person you are.

I love you. Always.

Forever yours,
